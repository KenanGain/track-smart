INSPECTION VIOLATIONS - DISPLAY NAME AND DESCRIPTION ONLY



Summary
=======

Display Name: Inspection Total
Description: Total count of distinct inspections during the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Inspection Violation Total
Description: Total count of violations during the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Inspection Violation Ratio
Description: Percent (ratio) of violations to inspections in the rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Inspection Violation Ratio Group
Description: Based on an entity's inspection violation ratio, each entity is assigned a violation pain point group to indicate the relative severity based of their violation ratio compared to the distribution of violation ratios among active entities: A. No Violations; B. Very Low; C. Low; D. Med; E. High; U. No Inspections.


Hours-of-Service
================

Description: Total count of all Hours of Service related violations in the last rolling 24 months.

Accident Summary
================

Display Name: Accident Total
Description: Total count of accidents in the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Accident Ratio (Est.)
Description: Estimated accident ratio is the total accidents per 100 estimated power units. Calculation includes accidents in the last rolling 24 months, averaged over 12 months to get an annualized value.

Display Name: Accident Ratio (Est.) Group
Description: Based on the estimated accident to power unit ratio, each entity is assigned an accident pain point group to indicate the relative severity of their accident ratio compared to the distribution of accident ratios among active entities: A. High; B. Med; C. Low.

Display Name: Accidents w/Fatalities
Description: Total count of accidents involving a fatality in the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Accidents Total Fatalities
Description: Total fatality count in the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Accidents w/Injuries
Description: Total count of accidents involving an injury in the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Accidents Total Injuries
Description: Total injury count in the last 




rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Accidents w/Property Damage
Description: Total count of accidents involving property damage in the last rolling 24 months.

Display Name: Accidents w/Tow-Away
Description: Total count of accidents involving a tow-away in the last 24 months.

Display Name: Accidents w/Rollover
Description: Total count of rollover accidents in the last 24 months.
